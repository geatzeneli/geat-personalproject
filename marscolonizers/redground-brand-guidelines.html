01 — Brand Identity

Who We Are

Redground is Mars's first and only native-grown coffee chain. Founded in the pressurized agri-domes of Olympus City's Lower Ring, we source our beans from hydroponic dark-roast farms tucked inside Tharsis crater networks — grown under LED-tuned spectrums that mimic an Earth dawn no living Martian has ever seen.

We are not nostalgic. We are not a reminder of Earth. We are what coffee becomes when it grows somewhere harder.

"We exist to give the people of Mars a ritual that belongs to them — not borrowed, not replicated, not shipped at 20-minute signal delay. Redground is yours."

Unique Value Proposition

01

Grown on Mars

100% hydroponic cultivation inside pressurized basalt-walled domes. Zero import tariffs. Zero Earth customs lag. No supply chain that could be severed by a dust storm in transit orbit.

02

Low-G Brewing

Our proprietary 0.38g extraction method produces a crema density impossible to replicate on Earth. Every espresso is a literal product of Martian gravity — a sensory experience exclusive to this planet.

03

Dome-First Design

Every Redground location is architecturally embedded into its dome neighborhood. No two look alike. The Hellas Basin cafe is carved into the crater wall. Olympus Mons Summit has transparent ceilings.

Tone of Voice

Our voice sits at the intersection of frontier pragmatism and quiet pride. We don't shout. We don't romanticize. We speak like someone who's earned their place on a difficult planet.

We are
Understated. Confident. Bone-dry in humor. We treat our customers as capable adults who signed a multi-year colony contract.
We are not
Nostalgic for Earth. Precious about "artisan culture." Overly aspirational. Or ever, ever, preachy about sustainability.
On product
Precise and sensory. We describe taste, texture, and experience with the care of a geologist describing a rock formation.
On community
Warm but not sentimental. We know the colony is hard. We're just here making it slightly more worth staying.
02 — Logo & Visual Identity

The Mark

The Redground logo is built around a fractured planetary circle — the "Breach" — representing Mars's volcanic past, the break from Earth, and the pressurized integrity of a dome. It is asymmetric by design.
